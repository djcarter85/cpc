We hope to run similar events in the future from time to time. If you are…

  • someone within a larger church who could consider making a move for the gospel,
  • someone who could consider going to a more local church to better reach your neighbours,
  • someone already within a new church whose leadership are thinking about planting again
  • or someone who is just interested in praying for this work or finding out more,

…then we'd love to hear from you! You can get in contact with us via the Cambridgeshire Gospel Partnership website.
