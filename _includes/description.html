Run by members of the Cambridgeshire Gospel Partnership on 11 November 2023, the event was designed to give a vision to the next generation of church planters in Cambridgeshire.

In God's goodness, the last few decades have seen a number of new churches planted in the Cambridgeshire area from conservative evangelical churches. However, the harvest is plentiful and the workers are few, and there remain large areas of Cambridgeshire and beyond with relatively little gospel witness compared to Cambridge itself.
