A sinner saved by God's grace, Richard Coekin is married to Sian, a beautiful Welsh GP, and blessed with five children, three grandchildren and a permanently moulting dog.

Richard was for 29 years the Senior Pastor of Dundonald Church in South London, which grew from 35 adults and a few children through various stages to become a larger church of 800+ adults and 350 kids with nine church plants. God also used Richard to help develop the Co-Mission church-planting network of 30+ churches in London and to launch a variety of gospel ministries (including 9:38, London Men's Convention, a Parliamentary Bible Study) and some books (e.g. Gospel DNA, Our Father, Ephesians for You and The Reluctant Evangelist).

Richard is now Director of Reach Ministries UK, which seeks to encourage church leaders in effective gospel ministries through consultancy, mentoring, training and conferences.
